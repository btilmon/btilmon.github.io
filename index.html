Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the FOCUS Lab at the University of Florida with Dr. Sanjeev Koppal. My interests are computational photography and computer vision. I received a B.S.E. in Engineering Physics from Murray State University where I was an electrical engineering intern at Berry Global, a research assistant in the NDE Lab with Dr. Gheorge Bunget and a research assistant in the FOCUS Lab during the SURF Program.
