I am a Ph.D. student in the FOCUS Lab at the University of Florida where I research computer vision, computational photography and machine learning. I have previously been a research intern at Meta where I developed efficient machine learning depth estimation algorithms for AR/VR, and at NASA where I developed a simulator and neural rendering algorithms for a computational imaging sensor to better understand the geometry and reflectance properties of planetary surfaces. My favorite book at the moment is "Guns, Germs, and Steel: The Fates of Human Societies" by Jared Diamond.
