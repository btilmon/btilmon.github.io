I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Florida, where I am advised by Sanjeev Koppal in the Florida Optics and Computational Sensor (FOCUS) Lab. I am interested in computational photography, computer vision and machine learning. A large portion of my research involves developing hardware/software prototypes that combine deep learning and camera hardware.
