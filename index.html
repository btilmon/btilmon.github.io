I am a PhD student in the Florida Optics and Computational Sensor (FOCUS) Lab at the University of Florida. My research develops algorithms and hardware for learning to control new computational cameras. During 2021 I am fortunate to be interning in the Intelligent Robotics Group at NASA Ames for the summer and in Facebook Reality Labs for the fall.
