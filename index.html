Brevin Tilmon

Google Scholar | CV | Github | LinkedIn | Email


I am an engineer and researcher experienced in computational photography, computer vision and machine learning. I am currently a research intern at Meta working on computer vision and machine learning for augmented reality systems. Previously, I was a research intern at NASA where I worked on computational imaging, 3D computer vision and computer graphics. I am working towards my Ph.D. in the FOCUS Lab at the University of Florida.
